Privacy Policy

Holiday Adventure - Privacy Policy

1. Introduction
At Holiday Adventure, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

2. Data Collection
We collect the following types of data:

  • Error Logs: Information related to any errors or crashes that occur while using the bot.
  • Crash Reports: Detailed reports about the circumstances leading to a crash, including system information and bot performance data.
  • User-Submitted Issues: Any issues or feedback voluntarily provided by users.
We do not collect personal identifiable information (PII) unless it is explicitly provided by the user.

3. Data Usage
The collected data is used to:

  • Diagnose and fix errors and crashes.
  • Enhance the performance, features, and user experience of Holiday Adventure.
  • Analyze usage patterns to improve bot functionality.

4. Data Sharing
We do not share your data with any third parties. All collected data is used internally to improve the bot and is handled with strict confidentiality.

5. User Rights
You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: You can request a copy of the data we have collected from you.
  • Correction: You can request corrections to any data that is inaccurate or incomplete.
  • Deletion: You can request the deletion of your data within a reasonable time period. However, please note that deleting certain data may affect your use of the bot, and that we may not be able to accommodate deletion requests for data that has already been anonymized or aggregated.
To exercise these rights, please contact us via our Discord server: https://discord.gg/tSWTMdrMaV.

6. Data Security
We take reasonable precautions to protect your data from loss, misuse, unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction. However, please note that no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

7. Children's Privacy
Holiday Adventure is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so that we can delete the information.

8. Changes to Privacy Policy
We reserve the right to update or change our Privacy Policy at any time. Any changes will be effective immediately upon posting the updated Privacy Policy on the website. Your continued use of Holiday Adventure after we post any modifications to the Privacy Policy on this page will constitute your acknowledgment of the modifications and your consent to abide and be bound by the modified Privacy Policy.

9. Contact Us
For any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via our Discord server: https://discord.gg/tSWTMdrMaV.
